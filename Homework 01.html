Homework#1

My favorite movie：Animal World

  • What is the movie about?

    • The protagonist, Zheng, is a clown who works in a game hall. His mother is seriously ill and in need of money for treatment immediately.
    To raise money, he sold his house with the help of his friends called Shrimp, but Shrimp set a trap when he signed the contract,
    cheated him of his money and made him sign a huge debt. Later, a mysterious man who claims to be a creditor, appears and invites
    the Zheng to board the ship for a gambling game. If he wins, he has the chance to pay off his debts and get a lot of money. If he loses, he will stay on the ship. The Zheng boards the ship and meets Shrimp again. They find that the loser
    will be caught doing
    human experiments. After experiencing deception and cheating among players, Zheng defeats other players, gains the opportunity to leave and
    earns a lot of money. However, under the temptation of money, Shrimp betrayed him again and tried to leave alone with money. Through
    his wisdom, Zheng took back his money and left the ship.

  • Why do you like the film?

    1. The film is adapted from Japanese manga, but it does not completely copy the content of the manga, simplifies the relationship between
      the characters and reduces the difficulty of the game, so that the audience can understand the whole story in 130 minutes.

    2. There's only one game: Rock-Scissors-Paper, but it's more difficult and interesting through multi-player and card games. I like this game
      very much, and I also like the feeling of analyzing with the movie.

    3. The special effects of racing are cool and the fighting scenes are wonderful.

    4. The choice between life and death, under the temptation of interests, is depicted very real. It transformed ships into a world of jungle.

    5. After betrayal and deception, the protagonist did not become an animal like others. As he said:

      “I'd rather be a life-long clown, and never be a jerk like you”.

  • Why should people see this film?

    1. The special effects and fighting scenes are cool.

    2. The game in the movie is very interesting, and the difficulty is just right. It's easy for the audience to think about it. It's also
      exciting to bet on life and death.

    3. The strategies used in the characterization of human nature and game play are reasonable. There are basically no big problems in the film.
